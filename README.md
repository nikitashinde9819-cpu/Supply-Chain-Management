# Supply-Chain-Management
This project focuses on analyzing end-to-end supply chain performance using SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau. The objective is to monitor key business metrics such as Revenue, Gross Margin, On-Time Delivery, Inventory Levels, Supplier Performance, and Logistics Efficiency.

Overview
The Supply Chain Analysis Dashboard is a data analytics project designed to evaluate supply chain performance using operational, logistics, and sales data. The project combines SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau to provide meaningful business insights and KPI tracking.

Business Objectives
Analyze overall supply chain performance.
Track revenue and profitability across regions and categories.
Monitor delivery efficiency and delay trends.
Evaluate supplier and carrier performance.
Improve customer satisfaction through logistics optimization.
Generate actionable business insights.

Problem Statement
Organizations often face challenges related to inventory management, delayed deliveries, logistics costs, and supplier performance. This project aims to analyze supply chain data and identify opportunities for operational improvement.

Tools & Technologies
SQL (Data Extraction & KPI Calculation)
Excel (Data Analysis & Dashboarding)
Power BI (Interactive Business Intelligence Dashboard)
Tableau (Advanced Data Visualization)
